MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art.

MonoMuse is more than just a museum — it's a celebration of human creativity across centuries. Our carefully curated exhibitions showcase works that challenge perspectives, ignite conversations, and bring together art, technology, and culture in one inspiring space.

From our founding in 2018, MonoMuse has welcomed over 1 million visitors. Our interactive installations span three floors and feature works from artists across 40 countries. We host over 50 events per year including workshops, lectures, and festivals celebrating the intersection of art and technology. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, there is always something new to discover at MonoMuse.
