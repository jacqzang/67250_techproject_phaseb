Membership FAQs

When does my membership begin?

Your membership begins on the date of purchase and is valid for one year from that date.

Can I upgrade my membership?

Yes! You can upgrade your membership at any time. The difference in price will be prorated based on your remaining membership period.

Are memberships tax-deductible?

Yes, a portion of your membership is tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for tax purposes with your membership confirmation.

What if I need to cancel?

Memberships are non-refundable. However, you may transfer your membership to another individual within your membership year.
